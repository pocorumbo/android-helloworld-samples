Saving Activity's per-instance state

onSaveInstanceState() is used for persisting state that is relevant to a particular Activity when it is destroyed and restarted. One such scenario is with you change device orientation while interacting with your Activity.

For persisting global state, ie. state that affects other Activities/components of your application, use onPause() instead of onSaveInstanceState(). Next sample application shows how to use it.
